Animate Objects

5th-level transmutation (ritual)
Casting Time: 1 action
Range: 120 feet
Components: V, S
Duration: Concentration, up to 1 minute
Casters: Wizard, Sorcerer, Warlock
Book: Player's Handbook

Objects com to life at your command. Choose up to ten nonmagical objects within range that are not being worn or carried.

At Higher Levels When cast with slots above 1st level, add another animated object.
